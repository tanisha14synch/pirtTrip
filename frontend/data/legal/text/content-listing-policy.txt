This Content Listing & Package Accuracy Policy governs all travel packages, listings, pricing, photos, descriptions, partner mini-pages, promotional material, and related content submitted to pirtTrip.com by travel business partners.
By submitting content to the platform, partners agree to comply with this Policy.

1. Purpose of This Policy
pirtTrip aims to maintain trustworthy and accurate listings for travelers while giving partners a fair platform to showcase their offerings.
This Policy helps reduce:
• Misleading offers
• Fake pricing
• Copyright misuse
• Fraudulent claims
• Spam listings
• Unsafe promotions
• Customer confusion

2. Allowed Listing Categories
Subject to approval, partners may submit lawful and genuine listings relating to categories such as:
• Domestic trips
• International trips
• Group tours
• Weekend getaways
• Trekking / adventure tours
• Customized travel packages
• Corporate outings
• Family vacations
• Honeymoon / leisure packages
• Related travel experiences approved by pirtTrip
Additional categories may be added or restricted over time.

3. Mandatory Pricing Transparency
Where pricing is shown, partners should clearly disclose relevant commercial terms wherever applicable, including:
• Price per person basis
• Taxes extra (if not included)
• Subject to availability
• Occupancy assumptions (such as twin sharing, if relevant)
• Date/season limitations
• Inclusions / exclusions
Ambiguous or misleading pricing may be rejected.

4. “Starting From” Pricing
Indicative pricing such as “Starting from ₹X” may be permitted where used honestly and with reasonable clarity.
Partners must not use unrealistic teaser pricing that is unavailable in normal circumstances.

5. Media & Image Usage
Partners may upload images only where they have the lawful right or authorization to use them.
Examples may include:
• Original business-owned photos
• Licensed supplier media
• Properly authorized promotional assets
Partners must not upload:
• Stolen images
• Copyright-infringing images
• Misleading edited photos
• Images falsely representing actual services
• Unauthorized competitor media

6. Accuracy of Listings
Partners are solely responsible for ensuring that all submitted content is accurate, current, and not misleading, including:
• Destinations
• Itineraries
• Hotel category claims
• Vehicle claims
• Dates
• Package duration
• Inclusions / exclusions
• Availability
• Visa/service support claims
• Contact information

7. Update Obligation
If material information changes, partners are expected to update the listing immediately or request revision promptly.
Outdated listings may be paused or removed.

8. Restricted Claims
The following claims may be restricted, rejected, or require proof:
• Cheapest / lowest price guaranteed
• No.1 company
• Best in India
• Guaranteed visa
• 100% safe
• Guaranteed confirmation
• Unlimited availability
• Risk-free travel
Unsubstantiated superlative or guarantee claims are discouraged.

9. Responsibility for Reliance
If a traveler relies on inaccurate partner-submitted listing information, responsibility for that misinformation may rest with the submitting partner, subject to applicable law.
pirtTrip is a platform facilitator and does not independently verify every listing claim.

10. Partner Mini-Pages / Business Profile Pages
Partners may be allowed dedicated profile or showcase pages on pirtTrip.
All such pages must comply with this Policy and must not contain:
• False claims
• Hidden fees
• Misleading credentials
• Competitor attacks
• Spam keyword stuffing
• Copyright violations

11. Original Content Requirement
Partners must not copy content from:
• Competitor websites
• Other marketplace listings
• Blogs without permission
• Third-party brochures without rights
Plagiarized content may be removed and may affect account standing.

12. Fake Testimonials / Misleading Social Proof
Partners must not publish fabricated reviews, fake testimonials, manipulated ratings, or false customer endorsements.
Any future review systems must be used honestly.

13. Prohibited Content
The following content is prohibited:
• Hate speech
• Illegal activity promotion
• Sexual or explicit content unrelated to lawful travel needs
• Political propaganda
• Dangerous stunt encouragement
• Violence glorification
• Fraud schemes
• Malware links
• Defamatory statements

14. Submission Review & Admin Approval
Listings may require internal review before publication.
Current operating model may include:
• Partner submits listing/package
• Listing enters review status
• Admin review conducted
• Approved listings are published
• Partner may be notified of status
No listing is guaranteed publication.

15. pirtTrip Rights Over Listings
pirtTrip may edit, reject, pause, delist, restrict, or remove any content at its discretion for reasons including:
• Policy violation
• Legal risk
• Customer confusion
• Outdated information
• Fraud suspicion
• Poor quality content
• Reputation concerns
This may occur with or without prior notice where necessary.

16. No Endorsement by Publication
Publication of a listing does not mean pirtTrip guarantees:
• Accuracy
• Value for money
• Availability
• Safety
• Service quality
• Partner reliability
Approval primarily reflects platform acceptance for display.

17. Audit & Clarification Requests
pirtTrip may request proof or clarification regarding claims, pricing, licenses, or content authenticity.
Failure to cooperate may impact visibility or account status.

18. Repeat Violations
Repeated violations may result in:
• Listing suspension
• Verified badge removal
• Connect restrictions
• Account suspension
• Permanent ban
