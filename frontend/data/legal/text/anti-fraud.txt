Effective Date: 8th June, 2026
Platform: PirtTrip.com
Operated by: Martyrs Services, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, India

This Anti-Fraud & Platform Misuse Policy explains how PirtTrip.com detects, investigates and responds to fraudulent, abusive, deceptive, or harmful conduct involving users, travel partners, customers, payments and platform activity.
By using the platform, you agree to comply with this Policy.

1. Purpose of This Policy
PirtTrip seeks to maintain a fair, trusted, and functional marketplace for travelers and travel businesses.
This Policy is intended to help prevent:
• Fake accounts
• Fraudulent leads
• Identity misuse
• Spam behavior
• Payment abuse
• Misrepresentation
• Platform manipulation
• Harassment
• Competitor sabotage
• Other harmful conduct

2. Scope
This Policy may apply to:
• Travel Businesses
• Prospective partners
• Travelers / inquiry users
• Employees or agents acting on behalf of users
• Third parties interacting with the platform

3. Prohibited Conduct by Travel Partners
Partners must not engage in conduct including:
A. Fake Leads / Self-Generated Leads
• Creating fake traveler inquiries
• Asking friends/staff to generate fake leads
• Manipulating lead systems for unfair gain
• Testing the system through false demand generation
Such conduct may result in immediate suspension or permanent ban.
B. Duplicate / Shadow Accounts
• Creating multiple deceptive accounts for the same business
• Circumventing prior suspension
• Using hidden identities to gain unfair advantage
PirtTrip may suspend linked accounts.
C. Fake Documentation
• Forged KYC documents
• Misleading registration papers
• False ownership claims
• Unauthorized use of another identity
Serious cases may result in permanent removal.
D. Misleading Commercial Conduct
• False pricing
• Fake discounts
• Non-existent packages
• False guarantees
• Misrepresentation of destinations or facilities

4. Prohibited Conduct by Travelers / Inquiry Users
Users must not:
• Submit knowingly false inquiries
• Spam multiple businesses abusively
• Harass partners
• Misuse contact tools
• Impersonate another person
• Use bots or scripts
• Submit malicious or disruptive requests
Repeated misuse may lead to restriction or blocking.

5. OTP & Contact Verification
PirtTrip may use verification tools such as OTP-based checks or contact validation measures to improve inquiry quality.
Even where verification is used, PirtTrip does not guarantee that every inquiry will convert into genuine commercial intent.

6. Customer Complaint Process Against Partners
If a traveler reports harassment, spam, abusive calling, misleading conduct, or repeated disturbance by a partner, PirtTrip may:
• Review the complaint
• Seek the partner’s explanation
• Issue a warning
• Restrict account privileges
• Suspend or discontinue partner access
Examples may include:
• Excessive unsolicited calls
• Repeated messaging after refusal
• Late-night disturbance
• Misleading promises

7. Customer Data Use by Partners
Partners are expected to handle customer data responsibly and in accordance with their own privacy commitments and applicable law.
PirtTrip does not supervise every downstream interaction once lead details are shared. Users should review partner policies and exercise judgment before proceeding.
Where serious misuse is evidenced, PirtTrip may still take platform action.

8. Competitor Sabotage / Bad-Faith Conduct
Any attempt by one business to damage another through:
• Fake inquiries
• False complaints
• Review manipulation
• Misleading reports
• Coordinated disruption
may result in investigation and enforcement action.

9. Payment Abuse / Chargeback Misuse
If a partner purchases Connects or services and then engages in dishonest payment disputes, abusive chargebacks, or bad-faith reversal attempts, PirtTrip may:
• Freeze account access
• Pause services
• Withhold benefits
• Recover dues where lawful
• Terminate account

10. Evidence Sources We May Use
PirtTrip may rely on available evidence such as:
• Internal analytics
• Platform behavior signals
• Complaint records
• Payment records
• Screenshots
• Communication samples voluntarily provided
• Document review results
• Historical account patterns
We are not obligated to disclose internal detection methods.

11. Immediate Action Without Prior Notice
Where urgent risk, fraud suspicion, legal exposure, or safety concerns exist, PirtTrip may take immediate action without prior notice, including:
• Temporary hold
• Suspension
• Lead restriction
• Listing removal
• Investigation lock

12. Connect Confiscation / No Refund in Abuse Cases
If fraud, manipulation, or serious misuse is found, PirtTrip may revoke benefits including:
• Connect balances
• Promotional credits
• Verified badges
• Account privileges
Refunds may be denied in such cases.

13. Investigations & Cooperation Duty
Users and partners are expected to cooperate reasonably during investigations, including clarification requests and supporting information.
Failure to cooperate may influence business decisions.

14. Law Enforcement & Legal Requests
Where legally required or reasonably necessary in serious matters, PirtTrip may cooperate with:
• Police authorities
• Courts
• Regulators
• Payment processors
• Legal advisors
subject to applicable law.

15. No Public Naming Policy
PirtTrip generally does not publicly identify suspended or banned users/businesses solely for enforcement reasons, unless required by law or necessary to protect the platform.

16. Discretion & Final Decisions
Fraud and misuse often involve nuanced facts. Final actions may depend on risk assessment, evidence quality, repeated behavior, severity, and business judgment.
PirtTrip reserves discretion to determine appropriate platform action.

17. Appeals / Reconsideration
Restricted users may contact support for review. Submission of an appeal does not guarantee reinstatement.

18. Limitation of Guarantee
While PirtTrip uses controls to reduce fraud, no platform can guarantee the complete absence of misuse.
Users should continue to exercise independent caution.
