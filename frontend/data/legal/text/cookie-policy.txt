1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files placed on your browser or device that help websites function properly, remember preferences, improve security and understand usage patterns.
Similar technologies may include:
• Local storage
• Session tokens
• Pixels
• Tags
• SDK identifiers
• Preference storage tools

2. Why We Use Cookies

• Keeping users signed in
• Maintaining secure sessions
• Remembering preferences
• Saving consent choices
• Improving site performance
• Measuring traffic and engagement
• Detecting abuse or suspicious activity
• Supporting future advertising or remarketing features
